# Library that support mean square error optimization



#### RMSE, MSE, R-squared (Synonyms: **L2 loss**)

- Tree-based 

  > `XGBoost`, `LightGBM`
  > `sklearn.RandomForestRegressor`

- Linear models

  > `sklearn.<>Regression`
  >
  > `sklearn.SGDRegressor`
  >
  > `Vowpal Wabbit` (quantile loss)

- Neural nets

  > `PyTorch`, `Keras`, `TF`, etc.

- Linear models

  >
  > ~~`sklearn.SGDRegressor`~~
